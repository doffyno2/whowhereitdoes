<h2>What is Sodium?</h2>

Sodium is an essential mineral that is found in many foods and beverages. It is an electrolyte that helps to regulate blood pressure, muscle contractions, and fluid balance in the body. Too much sodium can lead to high blood pressure, stroke, and heart disease. The American Heart Association recommends no more than 2,300 mg of sodium per day for most adults and 1,500 mg of sodium per day for those with high blood pressure. 

<h2>How Much Sodium is in Beer?</h2>

The amount of sodium in beer varies between different brands and types of beer. Generally, regular beer contains an average of 8 to 10 milligrams of sodium per 12-ounce serving. Light beers tend to contain less sodium than regular beers, ranging from 5 to 8 milligrams per 12-ounce serving.

Drinking beer, in moderation, can provide numerous health benefits.
