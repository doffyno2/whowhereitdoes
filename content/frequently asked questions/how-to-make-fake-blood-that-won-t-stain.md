<h2>Using Faux Blood</h2>

<p>Faux blood is a safe, non-toxic alternative to real blood. It is created by mixing corn syrup, red food coloring, and either cocoa powder or cornstarch. The mixture is heated until it thickens, then cooled and stored. Faux blood has the same appearance as real blood, but it won’t stain clothes or furniture because it is non-toxic and water-soluble. The only downside is that it doesn’t have the same texture as real blood.</p>
