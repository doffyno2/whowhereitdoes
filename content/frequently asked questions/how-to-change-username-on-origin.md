<h2>How to Change Username on Origin</h2>

<p>Changing your Origin username is a fairly straightforward process. Here’s how to do it:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Open the Origin client and log in. </li>
  <li>Click on your username, which will be displayed in the top right corner of the screen. </li>
  <li>In the drop-down menu, click on “Account Settings.” </li>
  <li>Select “Profile” from the left-hand menu. </li>
  <li>Enter your new username in the field provided. </li>
  <li>Click “Save” to apply the changes. </li>
</ul>

<p>Your new Origin username will be applied immediately, and you can start using it right away.</p>
