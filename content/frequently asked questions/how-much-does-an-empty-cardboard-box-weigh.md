<h2>What Is a Cardboard Box?</h2>

<p>A cardboard box is a type of packaging made from paper or other fibrous material that is designed to protect goods during transportation. Cardboard boxes are typically made from corrugated cardboard, which is a type of paperboard that consists of two flat layers with a wavy fluted core in between. This type of cardboard is strong and lightweight, making it ideal for packaging and shipping goods.</p>
